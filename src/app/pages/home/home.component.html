Discover more about your favorite Pokémon!

The Pokédex is a digital tool that serves as an encyclopedia within the world of Pokémon, used by trainers and characters to obtain details about the different Pokémon species. In most video games, apps, and platforms that belong to the Pokémon franchise, you can access the Pokédex from the navigation menu or main interface, and by clicking on it, you can check the information of any Pokémon you have encountered or captured during your adventure.
